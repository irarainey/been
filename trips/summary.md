# 2024 Travel Highlights

## ![France](/resources/flags/fr.png) Les Villards-sur-Thônes and La Clusaz, France (February 6-8, 2024)
A three-day ski trip to the French Alps with friend Steve, marked by stunning alpine scenery and cozy mountain villages. Starting in Les Villards-sur-Thônes, the tranquility and beauty of the snow-covered peaks set a serene scene for enjoying a locally crafted beer amidst the crisp mountain air. A stop in La Clusaz brought a blend of picturesque architecture and the lively atmosphere of alpine culture, with its warmly lit stone buildings and welcoming establishments. The trip concluded back on the slopes of Les Villards-sur-Thônes, where the serene, snow-laden ski runs framed by misty skies and towering evergreens provided the perfect end to this winter escape.

## ![Denmark](/resources/flags/dk.png) Copenhagen, Denmark (May 27-28, 2024)
Part of a larger Scandinavian adventure by train that also included Sweden and Norway, this stop in Copenhagen encapsulated the charm of the Danish capital. The vibrant waterfront of Nyhavn offered iconic colorful townhouses and historic ships along the canal, a true snapshot of the city's beauty. A later pause outdoors indulging in local beers showcased the city's cozy atmosphere. Finally, a visit to Rosenborg Castle with its striking architecture, lush gardens, and imposing spires highlighted Copenhagen’s rich historical and cultural heritage.

## ![Hungary](/resources/flags/hu.png) Budapest, Hungary (November 10-12, 2024)
A memorable trip to Budapest with old friend Paul, exploring the city's iconic sights. From the warm glow of a cozy bar to a misty day at Fisherman's Bastion, the city offered rich historical and cultural experiences. Walking along the Danube provided unforgettable views of Buda Castle and the Chain Bridge, while Heroes' Square stood as a testament to Hungary's storied past.
